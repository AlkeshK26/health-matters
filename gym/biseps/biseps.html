HAMMER CURL

Steps and Benefits of Hammer Curl

Steps:

  • Step 1: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand with your palms facing towards your body.
  • Step 2: Keeping your upper arms still, bend your elbows to curl the dumbbells towards your shoulders, focusing on contracting your biceps.
  • Step 3: Hold the dumbbells at the top of the movement for a brief moment, squeezing your biceps.
  • Step 4: Lower the dumbbells back to the starting position, keeping control throughout the movement.
  • Step 5: Repeat for the desired number of reps and sets, increasing the weight or reps as you build strength and endurance.

Benefits:

  • Targets the biceps brachii muscles, specifically the outer bicep.
  • Works the brachialis muscle, assisting in elbow flexion.
  • Improves overall biceps strength, size, and development.
  • Enhances athletic performance and reduces the risk of elbow injuries.
  • Can help improve grip strength and overall upper body strength.
  • Is a versatile exercise that can be performed with a variety of equipment, including dumbbells, barbells, and cables.
  • Allows for a range of grip widths and stances, making it suitable for different training goals and preferences.
  • Can be performed seated or standing, making it accessible for those with mobility limitations.
