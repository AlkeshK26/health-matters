Weight Gain Diet Plan and Workout Routine

Diet Plan

To gain weight, you need to consume more calories than you burn. Aim to increase your daily caloric intake by 250-500 calories to start, and adjust as needed.

Macronutrient Breakdown

  • Protein: 1.6-2.2 grams per kilogram of body weight
  • Carbohydrates: 2-3 grams per kilogram of body weight
  • Fat: 0.5-1 gram per kilogram of

Sample Meal Plan:

Breakfast:

Oatmeal with banana, almond butter, and eggs (400 calories, 30g protein, 60g carbs, 20g fat)

Snack:

Greek yogurt with honey and nuts (200 calories, 20g protein, 30g carbs, 10g fat)

Lunch:

Grilled chicken breast with brown rice and vegetables (500 calories, 40g protein, 60g carbs, 20g fat)

Snack:

Apple slices with peanut butter (150 calories, 8g protein, 20g carbs, 8g fat)

Dinner:

Beef stir-fry with brown rice and mixed vegetables (600 calories, 50g protein, 80g carbs, 30g fat)

Before Bed Snack:

Cottage cheese with fruit (200 calories, 20g protein, 30g carbs, 10g fat)

Home Workout:

Aim to do 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps for each exercise, 3-4 times a week.

Upper Body:

  • Push-ups
  • Incline dumbbell press
  • Bent over dumbbell rows
  • Tricep dips (using a chair or bench)

    • Lower Body:

  • Squats
  • Lunges
  • Calf raises
  • Leg press (using body weight or dumbbells)

    • Core:

  • Plank
  • Russian twists (using a medicine ball)
  • Leg raises (hanging leg raises or using a bench)

    • Gym Workout:
