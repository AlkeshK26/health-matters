Weight Loss Diet Plan and Workout Routine

Diet Plan

To lose weight, you need to consume fewer calories than you burn. Aim to decrease your daily caloric intake by 500-1000 calories to start, and adjust as needed.

Macronutrient Breakdown

  • Protein: 1.2-1.6 grams per kilogram of body weight
  • Carbohydrates: 1.5-2 grams per kilogram of body weight
  • Fat: 0.5-1 gram per kilogram of body weight

Sample Meal Plan

Breakfast:

Oatmeal with fruit and nuts (300 calories, 20g protein, 40g carbs, 15g fat)

Snack:

Greek yogurt with berries and honey (150 calories, 15g protein, 30g carbs, 10g fat)

Lunch:

Grilled chicken breast with brown rice and mixed vegetables (400 calories, 35g protein, 50g carbs, 20g fat)

Snack:

Carrot sticks with hummus (100 calories, 5g protein, 20g carbs, 10g fat)

Dinner:

Baked salmon with quinoa and steamed broccoli (500 calories, 40g protein, 60g carbs, 25g fat)

Before Bed Snack:

Cottage cheese with cucumber slices (150 calories, 20g protein, 10g carbs, 10g fat)

Workout Routine

Home Workout:

Aim to do 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps for each exercise, 3-4 times a week.

Upper Body:

  • Push-ups
  • Incline dumbbell press
  • Bent over dumbbell rows
  • Tricep dips (using a chair or bench)

Lower Body:

  • Squats
  • Lunges
  • Calf raises
  • Leg press (using body weight or dumbbells)

Core:

  • Plank
  • Russian twists (using a medicine ball)
  • Leg raises (hanging leg raises or using a bench)

Gym Workout:

Aim to do 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps for each exercise, 5-6 times a week.

Upper Body:

  • Push-ups
  • Incline dumbbell press
  • Bent over dumbbell rows
  • Tricep dips (using a chair or bench)

  • Squats
  • Lunges
  • Calf raises
  • Leg press (using body weight or dumbbells)

    • Core:

  • Plank
  • Russian twists (using a medicine ball)
