Fitness and Health Plan

Fitness Plan

Congratulations on having a normal BMI index! To maintain your fitness and health, we recommend the following plan:

  • Exercise regularly: Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic exercise, or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic exercise, or a combination of both, per week.
  • Incorporate strength training: Include exercises that work all major muscle groups, such as legs, hips, back, chest, shoulders, and arms, at least two times per week.
  • High-intensity interval training (HIIT): Incorporate HIIT workouts, which involve short bursts of high-intensity exercise followed by brief periods of rest, to improve cardiovascular health and burn calories.
  • Flexibility and stretching: Incorporate stretching exercises to improve flexibility and range of motion.

Health Tips

In addition to regular exercise, here are some health tips to help you maintain your fitness and health:

  • Eat a balanced diet: Focus on whole, unprocessed foods, such as fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats.
  • Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water throughout the day to stay hydrated and support overall health.
  • Get enough sleep: Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep per night to help your body recover from the day's activities.
  • Manage stress: Engage in stress-reducing activities, such as meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises, to help manage stress and anxiety.

Workout Routine

Here is a sample workout routine that you can follow:

Aim to do 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps for each exercise, 3-4 times a week.

Upper Body:

  • Bench press
  • Incline dumbbell press
  • Bent over barbell rows
  • Tricep pushdowns

    • Lower Body:

  • Squats
  • Leg press
  • Lunges
  • Leg extensions

    • Core:

  • Plank
  • Russian twists (using a medicine ball)
  • Leg raises (hanging leg raises or using a bench)
